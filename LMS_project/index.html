            An LMS, or Learning Management System, is a software application used to administer, 
        document, track, report on, and deliver online courses and training programs.

        It provides a centralized platform for organizations and educational institutions to manage
        all aspects of their e-learning activities,including creating course content, assigning tasks, 
        tracking progress,and assessing performance.
        
        LMS platforms can be used for corporate training, academic education,and professional 
        development, supporting both remote and in-person learning.
